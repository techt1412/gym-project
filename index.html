Why Choose Us?

At Gravity YOUR HEALTH IS OUR PRIORITY. Gravity is one of a kind Gym with warm, positive and inviting ambiance, top of the class equipments, Qualified and friendly professional staff, clean, stylish and comfortable hassle free facility.

The first step towards developing an effective fitness regimen is to understand a client completely. We follow a balanced approach when it comes to fitness training and diet planning. Our fitness plan integrates all the 5 fitness components, Cardiovascular Endurance, Muscular Endurance, Muscular-Skeletal strength, Flexibility and Ideal Body Composition along with a holistic diet plan to help you rejuvenate mind, body and soul. We apply a holistic, personalized approach and focus on the root cause to help you lose weight, manage stress, age gracefully as well as manage various health concerns.

Our professional staff comprise of Qualified in-house Physiotherapists, Certified trainers and Qualified nutritionist and Natural Health counselor.
